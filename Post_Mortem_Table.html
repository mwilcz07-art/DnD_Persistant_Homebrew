STEP 1 — DESTRUCTION SEVERITY

Immediately upon death, roll 1d6 to determine overall bodily damage. This roll replaces DM hand-picking unless a spell or effect explicitly destroys the body (Rule I), or the DM chooses to override.

1d6 Roll Destruction Severity
1Minimal Damage
2Moderate Damage
3Severe Damage
4Critical Damage
5Extreme Damage
6Near-Annihilation

STEP 2 — BODY PART SURVIVAL

For each body part, roll 1d20 and compare the result to the survival threshold for the current destruction severity. If the roll meets or exceeds the listed number, the body part survives. If it fails, that body part is destroyed.
